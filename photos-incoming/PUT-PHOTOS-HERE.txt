Drop your photos into THIS folder, then run  optimize-photos.py.

  • Any format works: JPG, PNG, iPhone HEIC, WEBP, TIFF.
  • They'll be shrunk, compressed, and published to the website's
    "THE HUB IN ACTION" gallery automatically.
  • To control order, prefix names with numbers:  01_river.jpg, 02_team.jpg
  • The caption on the site comes from the file name, so name them nicely:
    "shred-the-debt-launch.jpg"  ->  "Shred The Debt Launch"
