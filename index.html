The Network

Global Shapers

Born out of the World Economic Forum, the Global Shapers Community is a network of young people under 30 who believe in a world where youth are at the heart of solution-building, policy-making, and lasting change. In 2024–25, Shapers delivered 800+ projects reaching 2.2 million people.
